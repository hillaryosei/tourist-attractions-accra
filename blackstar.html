Black Star Square is a public space, surrounded by the Accra Sports Stadium and the Kwame Nkrumah Memorial Park. It was built in 1961, around the time of Queen Elizabeth II's visit to Ghana. The square regularly hosts celebrations commemorating Ghana's independence from Great Britain in 1957, as well as other national events. It is also the site for all civic and military parades in Ghana.
