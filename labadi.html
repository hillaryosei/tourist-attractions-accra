Labadi Beach, aka La Pleasure Beach is the busiest beach on Ghana's coast. Labadi Beach is in a town called La, popularly known as Labadi, near Teshie in the Greater Accra Region of Ghana. On weekends and holidays, there are usually reggae, hiplife, playback, and cultural drumming/dancing performances. Aside from visiting the beach to have fun, people visit the beach early in the morning to get their steps in.
